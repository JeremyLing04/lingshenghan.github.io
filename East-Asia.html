Taste the Trend

Budae Jjigae

Budae Jjigae is popular in Korea for its hearty blend of spicy broth and a mix of American and Korean ingredients, symbolizing post-war creativity and comfort food.

Oyster Omelet

Oyster Omelet (Ke-á Chian) is a popular Taiwanese street food made with fresh oysters, eggs, and a mix of vegetables, all cooked together into a savory, slightly gooey omelet.

Japanese Soufflé Pancakes

Japanese Soufflé Pancakes are fluffy, cloud-like pancakes known for their light, airy texture and high, puffy height, resulting in a delicate, melt-in-your-mouth experience.
