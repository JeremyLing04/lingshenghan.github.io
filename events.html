La Tomatina

Buñol, Valencia, Spain

La Tomatina is a food festival held annually in Bunol, Spain. Participants hurl tomatoes at each other for the pure enjoyment of it. The festival has been held since 1945. More than 40,000 people attend the event every year.And finally, you’ll need to bring your own tomatoes – around 200!

Taste of Chicago

Illinois, USA

Taste of Chicago is an annual food festival held in Chicago, Illinois. The festival features a wide variety of food from local restaurants, as well as live music and entertainment. It is one of the largest and most important food festivals in the United States, attracting more than two million visitors each year.

The Munich Oktoberfest

Munich, Germany

The core activity of the Oktoberfest is naturally drinking, especially the special brews provided by the six major local breweries in Bavaria for the festival. The alcohol content of these beers is usually slightly higher than usual. In addition to beer, the festival also includes a wealth of traditional Bavarian food, such as roast pork knuckle, sausages, roast chicken, pretzels, etc. In addition, the festival also features amusement facilities, music performances, traditional dances, etc.

Galway International Oyster Festival

Galway, Ireland

During the festival, visitors can enjoy a variety of fresh oysters and other seafood delicacies, especially high-quality oysters from Galway Bay. Plump oysters, fresh lobsters and delicious salmon, served with potato cakes and coated with traditional Irish cheese, are extremely delicious and have a lasting aftertaste. There are carnival parties, oyster tastings, oyster opening competitions that challenge the Guinness World Record, and grand oyster cocktail parties during the festival.

Tokyo Ramen Festa

Komazawa, Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo Ramen Festa is an annual food festival that celebrates the dish of ramen noodles. In addition to ramen, there are also stalls selling a variety of other Japanese dishes, as well as various drinks and snacks. With so many different options, the Tokyo Ramen Festa is the perfect place to sample some of the best dishes Japanese cuisine has to offer.

Salon du Chocolat

Paris, France

Salon du Chocolat is a food festival that celebrates chocolate in all its forms. Held annually in Paris since 1996, the event has always attracted some of the world’s leading chocolatiers, who come to showcase their latest creations. Visitors can sample hundreds of different types of chocolate and participate in workshops and demonstrations.
