Why Green,

Green curry is a spicy Thai dish made with a green curry paste, which blends green chilies, garlic, lemongrass, and other aromatic herbs. The curry is simmered with coconut milk, creating a creamy, spicy sauce that's both fragrant and flavorful.

Taste the Trend

Nasi Ayam Penyet

An Indonesian dish with crispy, smashed fried chicken served with spicy sambal, steamed rice, and often accompanied by fresh vegetables and fried tofu or tempeh.

Ube Halaya Jam

A Filipino sweet treat made from creamy purple yam, known for its rich flavor and vibrant color. It’s commonly used as a topping or filling in desserts like cakes, pastries, and ice cream.

Banh Mi Sandwich

A Vietnamese baguette filled with marinated meat, pickled vegetables, fresh herbs, and pâté or mayo, creating a deliciously crunchy and tangy bite.
